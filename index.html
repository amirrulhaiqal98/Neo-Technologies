Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a laptop trade-in?

    Laptop trade-in is a process of exchanging your used laptop for credit or cash value.

  • How do I know the trade-in value of my laptop?

    Our trade-in experts assess the value based on factors such as the laptop's brand, model, age, condition, and current market demand. Visit our website or contact us for a personalized trade-in quote.

  • What should I include when trading in my laptop?

    Please bring your laptop along with its charger and any relevant accessories. It's also recommended to back up and remove any personal data before trading it in.

  • What happens to my personal data on the traded-in laptop?

    We take your privacy seriously. Before refurbishing or recycling traded-in laptops, we ensure that all personal data is securely wiped following industry-standard data sanitization procedures.

  • How do I receive the trade-in credit or payment?

    Once the laptop is evaluated, we will provide you with the agreed-upon trade-in credit or payment method, which can be used towards your new purchase or provided as cash or store credit.
